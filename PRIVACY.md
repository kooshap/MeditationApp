# Privacy Policy — Meditate

*Last updated: May 2026*

## The short version

Meditate does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Ever.

## The full version

**Data collection:** None. The app makes no network requests and has no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

**Data stored on your device:** Your timer preferences (duration, selected bells, volume) are saved locally using iOS system storage (`UserDefaults`). This data never leaves your device.

**Notifications:** The app requests permission to send local notifications solely to ring the ending bell when your session completes while the screen is locked. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on-device — no data is sent to any server.

**Permissions requested:**

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| Notifications | Rings the ending bell when the app is backgrounded or the screen is locked |

No other device permissions are requested or used.
